# 02 - How to buy CROWN using NOWPayments (USDT payment guide)

Effective date: 2026-02-21

CROWN is an internal digital credit used on the ROYAL platform.
Purchasing CROWN is completed with USDT using our payment processor NOWPayments.

This is educational content only and not financial advice.

---

Supported USDT networks on ROYAL:
- TRC20 (Tron)
- ERC20 (Ethereum)

Important: the network must match exactly on both sides (sender and recipient).

Confirmations:
- Your payment is credited after NOWPayments detects enough blockchain confirmations and sends a status update (for example: waiting → confirming → confirmed/finished).
- Confirmation time depends on the selected network and current congestion.

---

## 1) What you need before paying
You will need:
- an exchange account or wallet with enough USDT
- the ability to send USDT on a supported network:
  - TRC20 (Tron)
  - ERC20 (Ethereum)

Important:
- You must use the same network in checkout and in your wallet or exchange withdrawal.
- Using a different network can cause delays or loss.

---

## 2) Step-by-step: purchasing CROWN

### Step 1 - Open the purchase screen
- Log in to your ROYAL account.
- Open Buy CROWN (sometimes called Add Funds).

### Step 2 - Choose payment currency and network
- Select USDT as the payment currency.
- Select one network: TRC20, ERC20, or Polygon.

Choose a network that you can actually send from your wallet or exchange.

### Step 3 - Review payment details carefully
The checkout will display:
- the exact USDT amount to send
- the receiving address or invoice address
- the chosen network
- a QR code (if provided)
- an expiration timer (if shown)

Before you send:
- confirm the amount is exactly what you will send
- confirm the network matches your sending method

### Step 4 - Send USDT from your wallet or exchange
In your wallet or exchange:
- open Send or Withdraw
- paste the address from checkout
- select the same network
- enter the exact amount
- confirm

Do not type addresses manually.
Copy and paste.

### Step 5 - Keep the transaction identifier
After sending, your wallet or exchange will provide a transaction identifier (TXID).
Save it until your balance is credited.

### Step 6 - Confirmations and credit
After the payment receives enough confirmations:
- the system processes the payment
- your CROWN balance is credited

---

## 3) Troubleshooting and common issues

### 3.1 The payment shows pending for a long time
Possible reasons:
- blockchain congestion
- the exchange has not broadcast the transaction yet
- the payment requires more confirmations

What to do:
- wait until a TXID exists
- track the TXID on a blockchain explorer for the correct network
- if confirmed but not credited, contact support

### 3.2 I sent the wrong amount
- Underpayment may not credit automatically.
- Overpayment may not credit automatically.
Contact support with TXID and details.

### 3.3 I used the wrong network
This can be irreversible.
Contact support immediately with:
- TXID
- network used
- address and amount

### 3.4 Confirmed but not credited
Contact support and provide:
- your account email or username
- TXID
- amount, network, timestamp

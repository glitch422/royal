# 01 - How to buy crypto, set up a wallet, and purchase USDT (Tether)

Effective date: 2026-02-21

This guide explains, in practical steps, how to:
- set up a crypto wallet
- register on popular crypto platforms
- purchase USDT (Tether)
- send USDT safely using the correct network

This is educational content only and not financial advice.

---

Supported USDT networks on ROYAL:
- TRC20 (Tron)
- ERC20 (Ethereum)

Important: the network must match exactly on both sides (sender and recipient).

Confirmations:
- Your payment is credited after NOWPayments detects enough blockchain confirmations and sends a status update (for example: waiting → confirming → confirmed/finished).
- Confirmation time depends on the selected network and current congestion.

## 1) Security first (do this before any purchase)

### 1.1 Understand recovery phrases
A self-custody wallet gives you a recovery phrase (also called a seed phrase).
- The recovery phrase is the master key to your wallet.
- Anyone who has it can control your funds.
- Store it offline (paper or metal backup).
- Never share it with anyone, including anyone claiming to be support.

### 1.2 Protect exchange accounts
When using an exchange (a trading platform):
- Use a long, unique password.
- Enable two-factor authentication (an extra login step with an authenticator application).
- Avoid SMS-based verification if an authenticator application is available.
- Review login alerts and device authorization lists.

### 1.3 The USDT network rule (critical)
USDT exists on different blockchain networks. Common networks include:
- TRC20 (Tron)
- ERC20 (Ethereum)

When you send USDT, the sending network must match the receiving network exactly.
Example:
- If your receiving address is USDT on TRC20, you must send USDT on TRC20.
Sending on the wrong network can cause irreversible loss.

### 1.4 Test transfer best practice
When sending to a new address for the first time:
- Send a small test amount.
- Confirm it arrived.
- Then send the full amount.

### 1.5 Avoid common scams
- Use official websites and official application stores.
- Do not click unknown links in messages.
- Be cautious with “support” accounts on social media.
- Do not give remote access to your device.

## 2) Exchange vs wallet (what each one does)

### 2.1 Exchange (trading platform)
An exchange typically allows you to:
- register with an email address
- complete identity verification (Know Your Customer verification)
- fund your account using traditional money methods
- buy USDT
- withdraw USDT to an external wallet

Examples: Crypto.com, Binance (or Binance.US in the United States), Kraken.

### 2.2 Wallet (crypto address holder)
A wallet holds your addresses and allows you to send and receive assets.

Types:
- Custodial wallet: provided by an exchange. The exchange holds the private keys for you.
- Self-custody wallet: you hold the private keys. Examples include Trust Wallet and MetaMask.

## 3) Step-by-step guides for well-known platforms

### A) Crypto.com (exchange)
Goal: create an account, fund it, buy USDT, optionally withdraw.

Step 1 - Create an account
- Install the Crypto.com application from the official store.
- Register using email and a strong password.
- Confirm your email.

Step 2 - Secure the account
- Enable two-factor authentication via an authenticator application.
- Enable device lock and biometric security if available.

Step 3 - Complete identity verification (if required)
- Follow the identity verification steps shown in the application.
- Prepare a government-issued document and follow the selfie or live check instructions.

Step 4 - Fund your account
Funding methods depend on your region:
- debit or credit card
- bank transfer
- local payment methods

Step 5 - Buy USDT
- Open the Buy feature.
- Select USDT (Tether).
- Select the funding method.
- Confirm the order.

Step 6 - Withdraw USDT (optional)
- Open Withdraw or Transfer.
- Choose USDT.
- Paste your receiving wallet address.
- Select the correct network.
- Confirm and complete.

---

### B) Trust Wallet (self-custody wallet)
Goal: create a wallet you control and receive USDT.

Step 1 - Create a wallet
- Install Trust Wallet from the official store.
- Choose Create New Wallet.
- Write down the recovery phrase offline.
- Confirm the recovery phrase inside the application.

Step 2 - Secure your wallet
- Turn on a passcode.
- Enable biometric lock if available.
- Keep your device updated.

Step 3 - Get USDT
Two common options:
1) Buy in the application (availability depends on your location and available payment providers).
2) Buy on an exchange, then withdraw to Trust Wallet.

Step 4 - Receive USDT from an exchange
- In Trust Wallet, open USDT and choose Receive.
- Choose the network you want to receive on (for example TRC20).
- Copy the address.
- On the exchange, withdraw USDT to that address using the same network.

### C) Binance (exchange)
Goal: register, verify, fund, buy USDT, withdraw.

Availability note:
- Availability depends on your country.
- In the United States, Binance.com is generally not available. Users typically use Binance.US where supported.

Step 1 - Create and secure the account
- Register and confirm email.
- Enable two-factor authentication.

Step 2 - Identity verification (if required)
- Complete identity verification as requested by the platform.

Step 3 - Funding
- Add a funding method supported in your region.

Step 4 - Buy USDT
- Use the Buy feature or convert from your funded currency to USDT.

Step 5 - Withdraw USDT to your wallet
- Choose Withdraw.
- Select USDT and the correct network.
- Paste your wallet address.
- Confirm.

### D) Bybit (important note for United States citizens)
Bybit states that its services are restricted for users located in the United States.
If you are a United States resident, use a platform available in your jurisdiction, such as Kraken.

If Bybit is available where you live:
- the steps are generally the same as other exchanges:
  - create account
  - secure it with two-factor authentication
  - complete identity verification if required
  - fund account
  - buy USDT
  - withdraw to your wallet using the correct network

### E) MetaMask (self-custody wallet)
Goal: create a wallet and receive USDT.

Step 1 - Install from official sources
- Install MetaMask only from official sources.

Step 2 - Create a wallet
- Create a new wallet.
- Write down the Secret Recovery Phrase and store it offline.

Step 3 - Select the correct network
USDT may be received on different networks.
- Ethereum for ERC20 USDT
- BNB Smart Chain for BEP20 USDT (if you use it)

Step 4 - Receive USDT
- Buy USDT on an exchange.
- Withdraw USDT to your MetaMask address.
- Select the same network on the exchange.

Step 5 - Show the token (if needed)
Some wallets require adding the USDT token to display it.

Step 3 - Fund account
Funding methods depend on region and may include:

Step 4 - Buy USDT
- Use the Buy feature or trade from your funded currency into USDT.

Step 5 - Withdraw USDT
- Withdraw USDT to an external wallet using the correct network.

## 4) How to send USDT safely (summary)
1) Copy and paste the address.
2) Confirm the network on the receiving side.
3) Select the same network on the sending side.
4) If this is your first transfer, send a test amount first.
5) Keep the transaction identifier (TXID) for tracking and support.
